American Express rewards Cardmembers that regularly use Cards through our Membership Rewards program. The more you spend, the more Membership Rewards points you accrue. These points can be redeemed for over 1,500 reward options, including travel, shopping and entertainment rewards. There are three levels that you can choose from, depending on how you like to use your points.

Choices Ascent Ascent Premium

Reward Options 		Redeem points for over 1,500 options across a wide range of categories including: car hire, gift vouchers1, donations to charities, magazine subscriptions, unique experiences, family entertainment, credit on Card (consumers only)2 and merchandise.

Points for Travel 		Redeem points for travel through Travelscene American Express, Harvey World Travel3, Webjet4 and Membership Travel Services (online)5. Redemptions through Membership Travel Services (call centre and online) and Platinum Concierge6.

Guest Partners 		Ability to transfer points to selected frequent guest partner programs including: Hilton HHonors, Starwood Preferred Guest, Priority Club Rewards and Jumeirah7.

Frequent Flyer Options 		Ability to transfer points to selected frequent flyer programs including: Velocity, Virgin Atlantic, Malaysia Airlines, Cathay Pacific, Singapore Airlines, Thai International, Air New Zealand7.

Qantas Frequent Flyer Program 		Ability to transfer points to the Qantas Frequent Flyer Program8.
